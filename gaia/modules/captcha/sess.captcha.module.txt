Captcha (http://drupal.org/project/captcha)
----------------------------------------------------

Description
===========

Prevents creation of session for anonymous users. Side efect: anonymous users have to pass captcha test for every form submission (option skipping captcha after first successful submitions won't work).

Compatibility
=============

6.x-2.2 (last release).

==============
